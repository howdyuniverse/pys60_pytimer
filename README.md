# PyTimer
###### Countdown timer for pys60 platform


#### Controls
- 4 - stop (reset timer)
- 5 - start / pause
- 6 - set time

(on pause)
- 3 - plus 10 seconds
- 2 - plus 1 minute
- 1 - plus 1 hour

- 9 - minus 10 seconds
- 8 - minus 1 minute
- 7 - minus 1 hour


#### Dependencies
- [miso](https://github.com/contextlogger/miso) - builds: http://contextlogger.org/miso/download/
